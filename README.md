# Departmental Usage & Performance Dashboard  
### Excel | Data Analysis | Business Intelligence

---

##  Overview  
This project is a fully interactive **Excel-based Dashboard** designed to analyze **departmental usage**, **business performance**, and key operational metrics.  
It provides actionable insights into sales, clients, products, and profitability through visually engaging charts and KPIs.

The dashboard is built using **Excel formulas, PivotTables, slicers, charts, and custom UI design** to create a polished analytical report suitable for business presentations and decision-making.

---

##  Key Features  

###  **KPI Summary Cards**  
The top section highlights the main business KPIs:  
- **Sales (Total)**  
- **New Clients**  
- **Costs**  
- **Average Check**  

These KPIs give a quick snapshot of the organizationâ€™s overall performance.

---

###  **Influx of New Clients (Monthly Trend)**  
A combined line + bar chart showing the monthly variation in new clients.  
Useful for:  
- Identifying seasonal patterns  
- Understanding customer acquisition behavior  

---

###  **Regular Clients Gauge**  
- Visual percentage of regular clients.  


---

###  **Total Annual Profit (Yearly Comparison)**  
A triangular multi-year comparison chart that highlights annual profit differences.  
Supports insights into:  
- Long-term business growth  
- Best and worst performing years  

---

###  **Department Performance (Donut Chart)**  
Breakdown of key values across departments:  
- Eastern District  
- Northern District  
- Southern District  
- Western District  

Useful for regional performance tracking.

---

###  **Monthly Department Metrics (Clustered Columns)**  
- Displays **sales and costs** across months and departments.  

---

###  **Product Categories (Radar Chart)**  
Visual comparison of product performance across categories including:  
- Bathroom  
- Kitchen  
- Garden  
- Decor  
- Living  

---

###  **Top 5 Products (Horizontal Bar Chart)**  
Shows the highest-performing products based on key metrics.  
Helpful for:  
- Inventory planning  
- Determining profitable products  
- Strategy & promotions  

---

###  **Average Check Trend (Area Chart)**  
- A month-wise trend chart showing fluctuations in **average customer spending**.

---

###  **Sales & Inventory (Bar Comparison)**  
A combined chart providing insights into:  
- Product category sales  
- Corresponding inventory status  

Essential for stock management and preventing over/under-stocking.
